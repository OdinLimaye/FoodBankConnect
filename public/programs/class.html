About the Program

The Nutrition & Cooking Class is designed to empower individuals and families with the skills to create nutritious, affordable meals at home. Each session is led by licensed dietitians and culinary experts who provide hands-on training, recipe guides, and strategies for meal planning on a budget. Participants also gain valuable knowledge about food safety, portion sizes, and dietary balance.

The program is income-based to ensure accessibility for those most in need, and costs are calculated on a sliding scale. Classes are hosted monthly at local community centers, creating a supportive environment where participants can share experiences and learn together.
